ABOUT ME

Alicia Chimeno Sarabia

Barcelona, Spain

Hello! I'm Alicia, a 23-year-old woman from Lleida. I'm a mathematician working as a generative AI engineer, passionate about #tech, #productivity, #personal-growth, and always seeking to expand my skill set in absolutely any area—from fitness and health to finances and politics. I'm obsessed with planning and always thinking about what’s next. I strive to make daily decisions that help me improve by 1% each day.

I created this website not to focus on myself, but to share the most inspiring messages I’ve learned throughout my journey, from books I’ve read and people I’ve listened to. My goal is to create a space that reflects a bit of who I am and what I love. No AI here, just pure motivation and passion. It's a place for reflection, with the hope of inspiring others.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or you simply share the same interests!
